Drug Cartel

"Rise to Power: The Drug Cartel" INTRODUCTION - Proposed game rules to follow: In the heart of the urban jungle, in a world defined by crime and power struggles, the infamous drug cartel has always ruled the streets. But now, in the wake of the sudden death of the former cartel leader, the cartel stands at a pivotal crossroads. In his last will and testament, the late leader left a challenging requirement: Only the one who can best navigate the streets, evade the long arm of the law, and reach a million dollars first, will be appointed as the new cartel leader. This is your chance to step into the dangerous world of drug trafficking, corruption, and risky decisions. Earn respect, build your empire, and seize every opportunity as you fight to fulfill the requirements of the will and become the undisputed ruler of the "Drug Cartel." Are you ready to take up the challenge and climb to the top of the hierarchy? Let the game begin, and show the world who is the true ruler of the streets!
